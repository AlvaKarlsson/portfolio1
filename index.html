"Don't limit your challenges. Challenge your limits."

Have you ever dreamt of completing an Ironman but don't have the tools to cross the finish line? Then you have come to the right place! We help you build a structured workout programme personilized just for you to succeed.

Who Are We?

We are two girls that want to help people around the world to dare the challenge of an Ironman. We are both certified personal trainers that specifies in triathlon but also help with strengh and conditioning that will help during your training. We know how hard it can be, because we both have completed different Ironman races and therefore knows what to do an not.

How does the programme look like?

We structure together with you a program that is compleatly suited to you and your personal level to make you as fit as possible until you're at the startline, both with nutrition and training. In the program there will be of course a lot of work on the bike, swim and run. But we will also implement some strenght work and ask you to complete some tests so we can se your fitness level from the start and the evolution during the program.
